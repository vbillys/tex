# Robot Overview Design

## Robot Base
- Differential Drive
- Three wheeled, two driven, one caster
- Carry up to 50 kg load

## Robot Upper Body
- Fixed chest, with two 4-DOF arms
- One pitch DOF head, (servo) with vision sensor (RGBD)
